Haodong Zhao (赵皓东)

I am a second-year Ph.D. candidate in Security Research Lab (Unicorn) at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Prof. Gongshen Liu and Dr. Zhuosheng Zhang . I am also supervised by Prof. Bingsheng He in National University of Singapore (NUS). I worked with Ant Group from 2022/10 to 2023/10.

I got my bachelor's degree from School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021, supervised by Prof. Gongshen Liu.

My general research interest lies in Federated Learning, Split Learning, Backdoor attack in ML and NLP.
