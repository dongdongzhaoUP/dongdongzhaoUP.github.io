    About Me

    • I am a third-year PhD candidate at School of Computer Science, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), supervised by Prof. Gongshen Liu and Prof. Zhuosheng Zhang. I am also supervised by Prof. Bingsheng He at School of Computing, National University of Singapore (NUS) and collaborate with Dr. Zhaomin Wu.
    • I got my bachelor's degree from School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in 2021, supervised by Prof. Gongshen Liu.
    • My general research interest lies in LLM Agent, AI Safety/Security/Privacy (Backdoor attack, Watermark) and Federated Learning.
    • I am selected for The CAST Youth Science and Technology Talent Cultivation Project (Doctoral Program, 2025), and host three joint projects with Ant Group from 2022/10 to 2026/4.
